About

Anime Fan Helper, helps our users find infomation about the anime they are intrested in. It shows TV, Movies, OVA, Specials, and ONA. It also shows other anime of simaler genre. Using the Jikan API. The site allows a user to "click find more" witch takes them to myanimelist.net.

How it works

Type in any anime you can think of and click enter. Then scroll down to see the results. Scroll over on any of the results to see more. All resultsare shown in alphabetical order.
