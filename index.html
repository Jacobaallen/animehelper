About

Anime Fan Helper, we are here to help the anime fan. Our search helps our users find information about the anime they are interested in using the Jikan API. We also have anime-related video games available. These games are free for the fans to use. Finally we have our review section. Where we will be reviewing different anime and manga.

How search works

Type in any anime you can think of and click enter. Scroll down to see the results. Scroll over on any of the results to see more information. All results are shown in alphabetical order. The site allows a user to click "find out more" which takes the user to myanimelist.net.
