About

At Anime Fan Helper, we are here to help. We are a multiple functions website offering multiple services. Our news serves brings you the latest in anime and pop culture related news. We also offer reviews on the hottest animation out there. Our search helps our users find information about the anime they are interested in using the Jikan API. We also have anime-related video games available. These games are free for our fans to use. All news story are both original and copyrighted of Anime Fan Helper, inc.

How search works

Type in any anime you can think of and click enter or search (may need to be clicked more than once). Scroll down to see the results. Scroll over on any of the results to see more information. All results are shown in alphabetical order. The site allows a user to click "find out more" which takes the user to myanimelist.net.
