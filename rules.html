Rules for Weekly and Monthly Raffles:

1. You must be a Patreon to enter. You must be 18 years of age or older to enter.

2. You can not win multiple weeks in a row. If you won one week, you will not be in the following weeks' drawing. (weekly raffle).

3. All eligible Patreon's are allowed to participate in the monthly raffle even if you won the month or week before.

4. Prizes will vary, but the most common prize for the weekly raffle is an official Anime Fan Helper sticker.

5. Upon drawing a winner, we will message the winner for his or her address to send the prize to the winner. You will have 5 days to message us back to claim your prize. If you do not respond within the time period, you will forfeit your prize.

6. We will cover the shipping and handling of the prize to the address provided by the winner.

7. Monthly prizes will be announced closer to the drawing.

8. All proceeds go to funding animefanhelper.com.
